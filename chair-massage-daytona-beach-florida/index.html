What is Chair Massage?

Chair massage is a convenient, fully-clothed massage experience performed while you're seated in a specially designed ergonomic chair. No oils, no need to undress, no lotion—just targeted, therapeutic pressure applied to your neck, shoulders, back, arms, and hands.

The therapist uses techniques such as acupressure, compression, and stretching to release tension in the upper body, improve circulation, and reduce stress. Sessions are quick—typically 15 or 30 minutes—making it perfect for people with limited time.

The Perfect Solution For:

  • Busy Professionals: Squeeze in relief during a lunch break or after work
  • First-Time Massage Clients: A low-pressure introduction to massage therapy with no undressing or oils
  • Anyone with Desk Job Tension: Neck and shoulder pain from computer work
  • Quick Stress Relief: Effective tension release in minimal time
  • Pain Management: Ongoing relief without a major time commitment

What to Expect

Stay Fully Clothed

You remain fully dressed in comfortable clothing. No need to change or undress. The therapist works through your clothes on your neck, shoulders, back, arms, and hands.

Seated Comfort

You'll be comfortably seated in a specially designed massage chair with built-in support. The chair is adjusted to fit your body properly.

Focused Work

Your therapist will concentrate on the upper body—neck, shoulders, upper back, forearms, and hands—using acupressure, compression, and stretching techniques.

Quick Session

Choose a 15-minute session for quick relief or 30 minutes for more comprehensive work. No lengthy process—arrive, sit, relax, and feel better.
