About Body & Foot Massage Spa Daytona

Family-owned massage spa dedicated to exceptional service and therapeutic excellence.

Our Story

Body & Foot Massage Spa Daytona is a family-owned and operated massage spa located in Foxboro Plaza on Beville Rd in Daytona Beach. For years, we've been dedicated to providing warm, professional, and therapeutic massage services to residents and visitors of Daytona Beach, Port Orange, and South Daytona.

We built our reputation on one core principle: exceptional foot massage therapy. Our therapists have a special gift for deep, powerful foot work that goes beyond the typical spa experience. But we've grown to offer a comprehensive range of massage services, each delivered with the same commitment to quality and client care.

Why Choose Body & Foot Massage Spa?

Specialty: Foot Massage

We're known throughout the region for exceptional foot massage therapy. Our therapists combine deep therapeutic technique with genuine care, making foot massage our signature service. Whether you're seeking pain relief, recovery, or pure relaxation, our foot massage therapists deliver results.

Professional Therapists

Our team consists of experienced, professional massage therapists who understand the human body and the power of therapeutic touch. Each therapist is trained in multiple massage modalities and brings genuine passion to their work.

Warm, Welcoming Atmosphere

Walking into our spa, you'll feel immediately at home. We treat every client like family. Our friendly staff and calm, peaceful environment make it easy to relax and enjoy your massage experience.

Full Range of Services

From our specialty foot massage to Swedish, deep tissue, couples massage, four-hands, chair massage, reflexology, and combo services—we offer therapeutic massage for every need and preference.

Walk-Ins Always Welcome

No appointment? No problem. We welcome walk-ins throughout our hours. Come in when it works for you, and we'll take excellent care of you.

Convenient Location

Located in Foxboro Plaza on Beville Rd, we're easily accessible from Daytona Beach, Port Orange, and South Daytona. We're perfectly positioned to serve your area.
