What Is a Couples Massage?

A couples massage is a unique spa experience designed for two people to receive massage therapy simultaneously in the same comfortable room. Our expert therapists work side by side, creating a synchronized, peaceful environment where you and your partner can relax together.

Same Room Therapy

Both clients recline side by side in a spacious, tranquil room with soothing music and aromatherapy.

Two Expert Therapists

Each person receives individualized attention from a dedicated, licensed massage therapist.

Synchronized Experience

Therapists work in harmony, creating a unified, deeply relaxing atmosphere for both clients.

What to Expect During Your Couples Massage

  1. Arrival & Check-In — Arrive 10-15 minutes early. Fill out a brief intake form noting any areas of concern.
  2. Meet Your Therapists — Our licensed therapists will greet you both and discuss your massage preferences.
  3. Enter the Massage Room — Relax in our warm, peaceful room with ambient lighting and calming music.
  4. The Massage Begins — Both therapists work in synchronized rhythm, creating a harmonious, deeply relaxing experience.
  5. Full Relaxation — Typically 60 or 90 minutes of uninterrupted massage therapy for both clients.
  6. Gradual Return — Therapists gently bring you back to awareness with a final shoulder massage.
  7. Rest & Hydration — Take time to ease back into your day. Drink water to aid recovery.
