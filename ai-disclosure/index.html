Artificial Intelligence Technology on Our Website

Body & Foot Massage Spa Daytona uses artificial intelligence technology to enhance your experience and provide immediate assistance. This page discloses how we use AI and what you should know about interacting with AI-powered systems on our Website.

What AI Technology We Use

Our Website features an AI-powered chat assistant provided by Telnyx. This assistant is designed to answer common questions about our services in real time, without requiring you to wait for a staff member to respond.

What Our AI Chat Assistant Can Do

The AI assistant is designed to help with the following:

  • Service Information: Describe our massage services, specialties, and what makes our practice unique.
  • Hours and Location: Provide our business hours and directions to our location at 1365 Beville Rd, Daytona Beach, FL 32119.
  • Pricing Estimates: Provide general pricing information for our services. Please note that pricing is approximate and subject to change.
  • General Questions: Answer frequently asked questions about massage therapy, relaxation, and our general approach to wellness.

What Our AI Chat Assistant Cannot Do

The AI assistant has significant limitations and should not be used for the following:

  • Book Appointments: The AI cannot reserve appointments or schedule your visit. To book, call us at (386) 281-3248.
  • Process Payments: The AI cannot accept payment or process financial transactions.
  • Provide Medical Advice: The AI does not provide medical, therapeutic, or health advice. It cannot diagnose conditions or recommend treatments.
  • Confirm Real-Time Availability: The AI provides general hours but cannot confirm real-time availability. Walk-ins are always welcome, but for guaranteed availability, please call ahead.
  • Collect Personal Health Information: Do not share personal health information, medical records, allergies, or other sensitive health data with the AI.

How AI Conversations Are Handled

When you use our AI chat assistant, please understand the following:

  • Conversation Logging: Conversations with the AI may be logged and stored for the purpose of improving the AI assistant and our customer service.
  • Data Processing: Conversation data is processed by Telnyx, our AI partner. Please review Telnyx's privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.
  • No Personal Health Data: We do not collect or store personal health information through the AI. Do not provide such information.
  • Service Improvement: We may use anonymized conversation data to identify common questions and improve how we communicate with customers.

All AI-generated content has been reviewed and edited by our team to ensure accuracy and alignment with our values and practices.

While AI technology is powerful, it has limitations:

  • The AI cannot understand complex or nuanced health-related questions.
  • The AI cannot replicate the personalized, human touch of direct communication with our team.
