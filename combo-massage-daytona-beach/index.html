What is a Combo Massage?

A combo massage combines two of our most popular services — body massage on the massage table and foot reflexology in our specialized foot chair — into one comprehensive session. Your session is split into two distinct phases: first, a full body massage on the table, then dedicated foot reflexology in our comfortable foot chair. You get the best of both worlds in a single appointment.

This is the ultimate value massage: two separate services, one session, one price. Whether you're dealing with foot pain, stress, muscle tension, or simply want the ultimate pampering experience, our combo massage delivers complete head-to-toe rejuvenation.

How Your Combo Massage Session Flows

We structure every combo session strategically to maximize relaxation and therapeutic benefit.

Session Structure Options

During your consultation, your therapist will ask your preference. Most clients enjoy the following flow, but we're flexible based on your needs:

Phase 1: Body Massage on the Table

Your session begins with you lying on the massage table for a comprehensive body massage. Your therapist works your back, shoulders, neck, arms, and legs using your preferred technique and pressure level. This phase targets overall muscle tension and stress relief.

Phase 2: Foot Reflexology in the Foot Chair

After your body massage, you move to our specialized foot chair for dedicated foot reflexology. This is a completely different experience — your therapist applies targeted pressure to reflexology points on your feet and lower legs, promoting deep relaxation and whole-body healing through your feet. The foot chair is designed specifically for this work, allowing your therapist to deliver precise, effective reflexology.
